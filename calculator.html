Population Growth Calculator

This is a population growth calculator that utilizes two different projection models: the exponential and the logistic growth models. You can use this calculator to find either the projected growth over a certain time period or the years it takes to reach a specific population goal. For population data, you can leave the "goal" input field blank and for the year, you can leave the "time" input field blank. The carrying capacity box is only for the logistic growth model.
